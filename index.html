The Adaptive Content Engine (ACE) Vision

Gamified Content Curation

Converts "boring" academic texts into high-interest, competitive formats (quizzes, short graphic novel scripts, viral video summaries) that **trick** middle schoolers into reading and learning.

Dyslexia-Optimized Format

Strictly follows accessibility rules: short sentences (10-12 words max), bolded keywords, ample white space, and **direct integration with Reading Progress data collection** for fluency tracking.

Secure, Compliant Delivery

Created by an actual classroom teacher, this solution bypasses public AI access bans by routing content through a local, approved server, ensuring **CIPA and FERPA compliance** while delivering the adaptive tools.
